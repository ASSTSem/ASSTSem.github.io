SCMOS > FinFET

Our SCMOS IC devices created in year 2001 with a newly defined basic switching element named CLtSBD(TM) which are embedded within CFET diffusion bed. a newly defined Logic and Memory circuitry were created such that multiple LtSBDs may be grouped to realize all CMOS circuitry with reusable SCMOS circuits, such that the on-chip network can be greatly reduced in bulk area and inter-wiring.

This innovative idea is very important and critical as it can overthrow Moore's law to replace all IC and IT system equipment with new implementations offering Speed,Power, Area, Yield, and Reliability(SPAYL) benefits. According to our analysis we can gain system advantages 16-160X over CMOS devices on each CMOS process node from 250 nm(Plane CMOS 2DTx) down to 14 nm(CMOS 3D FinFET).

Now that US is determined to rebuild the new IC manufacturing lines and IT assembly plants to lead the world again with TSMC engineers. We can work together to implement our SCMOS processes and circuits for the AI chips and modules of the G5 and G6 devices with much lower facility and labor costs. With our milestone plan for the 130 nm manufacturing line, networks can be much reduced and with better density and use 2D CMOS FET and the embedded LtSBD(TM) circuits may offer much higher speed operations than the CMOS 2D FET circuits. Once we proved our concept, then we can implement the 28 nm and the 20 nm node SCMOS devices. We can grow $T revenue with the 2D process lines to take over the IOE marketing.
